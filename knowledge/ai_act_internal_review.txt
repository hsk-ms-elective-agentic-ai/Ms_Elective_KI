Internal AI System Review — Talent Acquisition, confidential draft (June 2026)

System under review: "Resume Triage Assistant" — automatically screens and ranks
job applicant resumes before a human recruiter looks at them. In production for
our EU offices since March 2024.

Classification note (internal, not yet confirmed by Legal): under the EU AI Act's
Annex III, AI systems used in the "recruitment or selection of natural persons"
are listed as high-risk. The Resume Triage Assistant likely falls into this
category, since it materially influences which candidates a human ever sees.

Open compliance gaps identified in this review:
- No conformity assessment has been completed for this system yet.
- The system is not yet registered in the EU high-risk AI database.
- No human reviewer currently signs off before a candidate is auto-rejected;
  this needs to change to satisfy the human oversight requirement.
- Candidates are not currently informed that an AI system is involved in
  screening their application.

Recommended next step: pause auto-rejection (route all borderline cases to a
human recruiter) until the conformity assessment and registration are complete.
